Jack's Overview of the Sopranos

Tony Soprano

Anthony "Tony" Soprano is the boss of the DiMeo crime family, North Jersey's most powerful criminal organization. He lives in North Caldwell, NJ. Tony never had the makings of a Varsity athlete, small hands, that was his problem. This is what eventually lead to his downfall, along with the lack of roasted turkey in his fridge.

Phil Leotardo

Phillip "Phil" Leotardo is a captain of the more powerful New York City Lupertazzi crime family. Phil dislikes ice cream and is a reckless driver. Only the most seasoned and intelligable Sopranos fans, with extensive research, know that Phil did 20 years in the can.
